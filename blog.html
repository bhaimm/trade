Which is the best one for a business setup in Dubai?

Trade License in Dubai, UAE is issued by the department of economic development authority which permits the businessmen to do business in UAE. For certain categories of business, it needs approval from concerned ministries and other relevant authorities: For example, banks and other financial institutions must need permission from the central bank of the UAE, the insurance companies and related agencies need approval from the Ministry of Economy and Commerce, the manufacturing business requires permission from the Ministry of Finance and Industry and pharmaceutical and medical products should obtain from the Ministry of Health. All the commercial and industrial businesses in UAE should be registered with the Chamber of Commerce and Industry in the respective Emirate.
